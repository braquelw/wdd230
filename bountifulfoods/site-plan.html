Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote the Bountiful Foods business by providing information about the company and surrounding area. It will have information about the products, locations, and how to order.

Audience

This site is for anyone who is interested in nutritious foods. It provides an easy and convenient way to get healthy food into the pantries of our neighbors.

Personas

Amy is a busy fitness instructor and mother of 3 who wants to eat healthy but doesn't have to time to always make those choices. She loves the convenience Bountiful Foods provides with their nutritious fruit drinks and bags that provide her family with nutritious produce.

Peter is a laid back surfer from California who loves to support his local farmers and friends. He loves visiting the Bountiful Foods stores and picking up healthy lunch choices before hitting the waves.
