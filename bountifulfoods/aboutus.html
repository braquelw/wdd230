About Us

Bountiful Foods got their start when a few local farmers wanted to make their products more readily available to the general public. What started as one produce stand has now expanded to seven food trucks and a main storefront located in Carlsbad, CA. They have remained locally-owned and owner-operated to ensure the best quality and customer service. They not only provide fresh produce, but also offer fresh homemade juices and fruit infused water. Their mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options to everyone.
