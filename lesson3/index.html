Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to bring awareness and recognition to Hyrum City. It will provide relevant historical and present information about the town and events, along with a list of local businesses. It will include an option for a user to join the chamber, as well as incentives to do so. It will also have a contact page for users to get further questions answered.
