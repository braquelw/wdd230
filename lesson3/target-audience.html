Target Audience

The target audience for this site includes entrepreneurs and new potential businesses, existing business owners, community-minded people that want to influence the community, and pro-business members that care about the business environment of the city.

Personas

Anna

Anna is a 34 year old mother of three who owns a Salon in a busy part of town.

Both her and her husband lead busy lives as they hold down careers, raise a family, and are active participants in their community.

Anna is focused and driven with a business degree from a local community college and an active leader in her church. One of her biggest concerns is finding a good balance between her family, church, and career.

She spends her work time hiring and training employees, supervising staff and quality, and preparing marketing material to promote her business.

Anna is surrounded by a variety of people everyday, and no day looks the same in her world. She is constantly dealing with people, and knows a lot about the community. She is proficient on a computer and uses one to schedule, market, and research.

Quote: 'The key is not to prioritize what's on your schedule, but to schedule your priorities.'

Bill

Bill is a 65 year old recently retired Software Developer.

His life has drastically slowed down this past year and he is looking for ways to continue serving his community and those around him.

Bill is knowlegeable and passionate about the community he has lived in for over 30 years. His biggest concern is small businesses shutting down because of a lack of advertising.

He spends his time supporting local businesses and enjoying the sights and amenities of the community.

Bill interacts with a variety of people everyday, and loves to hear people's stories when they're willing to tell them. He knows a lot about the people and businesses in this community, and is always looking for ways to help others.

Quote: 'One of the greatest gifts you can give to anyone is the gift of your attention.'
